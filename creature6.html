Slit-Mouted Woman

Reiko Kashima, also known as the Slit-Mouthed Woman, is a yokai that formerly resided in a ruined area in Kamigoe City.

Creature Archive

Reiko Kashima is a tall female yokai, easily towering over average size humans, with muscular arms and legs. Her most distinguishing feature, which is where her epithet comes from, is her extremely wide mouth with rows of multiple sharp teeth. She wears a heavy amount of makeup; mascara that accent her round eyes and lipstick for her mouth. Her nails are also colored dark. She has very long, jet black hair, which is kept tied with a bow, and wears a crown on her head. When absent of her makeup, Reiko Kashima's appearance drastically changes. Her head is nicely shaped, and her facial features are revealed to be less prominent to where she looks more human; having normal eyes with long eyelashes and, most noticeably, a normal lipstick mouth. Overall, her face outside of her makeup has been noted to be remarkably beautiful by Momo and Jiji.

After choosing to wear less makeup, Reiko Kashima's dress became slightly altered from when she spent her time searching for Momo, with the only change being the loss of her puffy sleeves. She also lost her crown.

Reiko Kashima is a yokai who is vain and narcissistic, as she has an obsession with looking at her own reflection through her mirror and only favors things that are horrid since they only make her look good in comparison, which prompts her to trap them inside her mirror. The only grounds for her being willing to release her victims is if they block her reflection after taking up room and finds it even more dissatisfying when they "defile" her mirror by somehow acting cute. If someone were to offend her by calling her "ugly", and/or steal her mirror, Reiko Kashima loses her composure and breaks out into a highly agitated state at the offender. While she believes that she looks beautiful when she applies on her makeup, she seems to be rather oblivious of the fact that she is naturally beautiful when her true face is exposed.

For attacking her enemies, Reiko Kashima can project parts of her body from her mirror to other mirrors, or shards of glass, within her vicinity as a portal. Her main strength is the ability to remote attack with her mirror. This allows her to fight at a distance and to launch surprise attacks at her opponents by sending her hand through it and remotely striking them from anywhere glass is around.
