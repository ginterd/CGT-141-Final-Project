The Flatwoods Monster

The Flatwoods Monster is an alien and a UMA who was an associate of the alien group known as the Serpos.

Creature Archive

The Flatwoods Monster is a massive, heavily built 3-metre-tall alien (10 feet) with a build similar to a sumo wrestler, with a large belly and bulky, muscular arms and legs which it uses in combat. Its body is grey in color and has numerous lines that run down its sides. Its face that has circular eyes with prominent pupils, a rectangular mouth and an angular nose. Its overall head is not unlike that of a nutcracker. Its head was also adorned by a stocky helmet. Most notably, it possesses a strange hood that surrounds its head. In terms of clothing, it only wore a patterned tsuna. When its arms were destroyed, the Flatwoods Monster grew a pair of extra legs with a polka dot pattern on them.

As a subordinate to the Serpos, the Flatwoods Monster executed the tasks assigned to it. . In addition to its resemblance to a sumo wrestler, the alien also imitated the movements of one, and interestingly, its weakness for touching the ground with its hands aligns with the same sumo rule that dictates a sumo wrestler's defeat.

Full body of the Flatwoods Monster.



The Flatwoods Monster to scale.

The alien was first sighted in the town of Flatwoods in Braxton County, West Virginia and went on to become known as the "Flatwoods Monster". At some point, the alien came to Japan and had been reported to have been sighted in the Yamanashi Prefecture.

Some notable abilitys from the Flatwoods Monster is it's enhanced strength, and it's skills in Sumo wrestling. Befitting its immense size, the Flatwoods Monster had incredibly physical strength, being able to unleash shockwaves with a stomp of its feet and create craters with its punches.

The Flatwoods Monster unleashed its brute strength by using sumo style foot stomps. Since it fought in such a way, the Flatwoods Monster had the weakness of disintegrating if its hands were to touch the ground, being align with the sport's indication of a wrestler's defeat.

Other notable abilities include the ability to grow a new pairs of legs from its back in order to keep itself upright if its body were to disintegrate as a result of its hands touching the ground. The Flatwoods Monster possessed the ability to emit a noxious black mist from its mouth, or any open wound from its body, that caused nosebleeds to those who inhaled it. The affects of the mist is comparable to mustard gas.
