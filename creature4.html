Acrobatic Silky

Acrobatic Silky is a yokai that had been stalking Aira Shiratori ever since she was a little girl. She seeks to be the mother of Aira, despite not being blood related.

Creature Archive

Acrobatic Silky took on the form of an extremely tall woman with grey skin, long limbs and long silky jet-black hair. She had a narrow face with hollowed eyes, wrinkles on the upper half of her face and a permanent lipstick smile that exposes her big teeth. She had red nail polish both on her fingers and on her toes, her fingernails were long. She walked around barefooted while wearing a red wide brimmed hat and a red dress with the violet collar forming her long neck. Initially, Acrobatic Silky's face looked the way it did when she was alive until the day she had met Aira Shiratori.

Due to her past, Acrobatic Silky is a yokai who had a desperate need to find a child to love and call her "mother". Her main attraction was Aira Shiratori after becoming delusional in thinking that she was her mother during the time she was alive and was obsessed with having her all to herself. Acrobatic Silky's intentions also leaned towards her maternal instincts to protect the one she loves. Even if she is unaware of how that love is harming others.

Acrobatic Silky kidnapping Aira.



Close up of Acrobatic Silky.

As her name suggests, Acrobatic Silky is a yokai who possesses extraordinary acrobatic skills. Her exceptional speed, agility, and reflexes enabled her to timely react and dodge a swift rush attack from Okarun. Acrobatic Silky possesses incredible leg strength as she could easily send a human flying with a single kick and could release herself from Momo Ayase's psychic restraint.

The primary advantage of this ability was that it enabled Acrobatic Silky to extend her hair to considerable lengths and wield it at will. Her hair could entangle anything in its vicinity, allowing her to capture and fling her opponents. Additionally, it aided her in traversing challenging landscapes. A significant vulnerability of Acrobatic Silky's Acrobatics is its susceptibility to fire, which could lead to it being permed and complicate the process of untangling the objects she was attached to.

In conjunction with her acrobatic movements, Acrobatic Silky has been using Acrobatics to ensnare her surroundings. She can immobilize her enemies by binding and thrashing them, or employ her hair to seize objects and wield them as blunt instruments.
