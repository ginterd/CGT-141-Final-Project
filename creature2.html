Turbo Granny

Turbo Granny is a well-known yokai that was previously within a tunnel located at Shono City until her confrontation with Momo Ayase and Okarun.

Creature Archive

The yokai took on the form of a very elderly woman with reddish brown wrinkled skin, long thick white shaggy hair, wide eyes with a yellow sclera and were stuck looked to the sides. When possessing her targets, she has her victims transform into a mirror image of herself, albeit with a large mouth with teeth that is capable of biting off body parts.

Turbo Granny is a yokai that acts cruel, sadistic, and is extremely foul-mouthed. Since she possesses impressive running power, Turbo Granny takes great pride in her speed and refuses to be underestimated. When challenged to prove herself, she is not above using trickery to get her way, having been deceitful with Okarun and Momo in their game of tag by counting irregularly. Despite her negative attitude, she is revealed to have a compassionate side, as Seiko stated that Turbo Granny would most commonly wander off to spirits of girls who died tragically in order to comfort them.

Turbo Granny possessing someone.



Turbo Granny's spiritual form.

During modern times, the Turbo Granny was a yokai known as the 100 km/hr Granny that went around terrorizing the people of Japan. While wandering about, the Turbo Granny comforted the spirits of deceased girls who died horrible deaths and eventually fused with an earthbound spirit within a tunnel in Shono City and would curse those who would dare enter her territory by stealing their reproductive organs.

The major strength of Turbo Granny's extreme speed is that it enables her to transverse great distances. As noted by the yokai herself, she is able maintain a consistent running speed even while turning, giving her an edge over faster vehicles, such as a train traveling at 120 km/h. Turbo Granny also has the power to dematerialize her physical body into a spiritual form.

Turbo Granny's spiritual form's primary strength is her ability to possess her target's body, gaining complete control over their actions. Even while possessing someone else's body, she retains her other powers, especially her immense speed. Her possession applies not only to humans but also to spirits, as demonstrated when she took over the body of the Earthbound Spirit Crab in preparation to play a game of cat-and-mouse with Momo.
